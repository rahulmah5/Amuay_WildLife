About Amuay

For families looking to connect with nature and create cherished memories, Amuay Wildlife Park is an idyllic destination. Enclosed by the embrace of nature's untouched splendor, the park offers a playground for the young and old to discover the wonders of wildlife in harmony. Interactive trails lead to delightful encounters, from the jubilant chatter of birds to gentle deer grazing nearby, setting the stage for a magical family adventure under the lush canopy.

Amuay Wildlife Park is a peaceful haven away from the everyday, inviting families to immerse themselves in the richness of the environment. Sprawling across a lush landscape, it's a place where children's laughter mingles with the calls of the wild, sparking curiosity and wonder. A mere few hours' drive from the city's energy, our park is a cherished escape, nurturing love for the natural world across generations, where each visit contributes to the conservation of Venezuela's precious ecological wonders.
