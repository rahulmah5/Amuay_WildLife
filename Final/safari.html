Safari Adventures

Explore Our Safari Types

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the diverse ecosystems of Amuay Wildlife Reserve. Get up close and personal with the rich wildlife and immerse yourself in the beauty of our pristine natural habitats. Each safari is uniquely tailored to showcase the best of what Amuay has to offer, from the dense forest canopies to the sweeping savannas.

Dawn Chorous

Timing: 5:30 AM - 8:30 AM

Available: March - October

Experience the magic of the forest waking up. The Dawn Chorus safari is perfect for early birds and birdwatching enthusiasts.

$ 100 per person

Family discount: 25%

Jungle Trailblazer

Timing: 9:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Year Round

Venture into the heart of the jungle. Ideal for those who want to explore the depths of our tropical forests and its inhabitants.

$ 150 per person

Family discount: 15%

Savannah Sunset

Timing: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Available: May - September

Witness the breathtaking sunset over the savanna landscape. A romantic escapade or a tranquil family outing choice.

$ 250 per person

Family discount: 10%

Night Whispers

Timing: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Available: June - August (Full Moon Nights)

Unveil the secrets of the nocturnal wildlife. An exclusive safari under the glow of the moon, revealing the elusive night dwellers.

$ 300 per person

Family discount: 5%
